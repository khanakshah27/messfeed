Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a feedback?
Go to the 'Submit Feedback' section and fill out the form.
How long does it take to resolve a feedback?
It depends on the issue, but we strive to resolve complaints within 48 hours.
Can I edit my submitted feedback?
No, but you can submit an updated feedback mentioning the previous one.
Who can view my feedback?
Only the admin and authorized personnel can view your feedback.
